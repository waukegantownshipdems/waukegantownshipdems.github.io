Vote Local, Vote Waukegan!

Welcome to the Waukegan Township Democrats. We are your local grassroots, all-volunteer organization committed to fostering democracy and nurturing democratic values right here in our community.

Join Us

Our mission is engagement

From voter registration drives to community forums and town hall meetings, there are numerous ways for you to get involved and make a difference.

We value education

Active participation in the democratic process starts with information and ensures the representation of all voices and support for policies that benefit the whole of Waukegan Township.

Empower the next generation of leaders

We want every resident to take an active role in shaping the future of our township through advocacy, volunteering, and leadership opportunities.

Here, inclusivity isn’t just a word — it's action. Whether you’re a seasoned activist or new to local politics, your voice matters and there’s a place for you in the Waukegan Township Democrats.

Matt Muchowski - Chair, Waukegan Township Democrats
